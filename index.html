Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys are Alex Turner, Jamie Cook, Nick OMalley and Matt Helders. Formed in High Green, Sheffield in 2002, the band have released 5 albums to date, their most recent, AM released in 2013. The bands debut album Whatever People Say I Am, Thats What Im Not became the fastest selling debut album in UK chart history on its release in 2006. The release of subsequent albums Favourite Worst Nightmare (2007), Humbug (2009), Suck It And See (2011) and AM (2013) has consolidated their position as one of the most successful British bands to emerge in recent times. They are the only band to have won both Best British Group and British Album of The Year at The Brits three times. The band has now won 7 Brit awards out of 9 nominations since their debut album in 2006. Other achievements include an Ivor Novello award, a Mercury Music Prize, 20 NME awards, 5 Q awards and 3 Grammy nominations.
